3 Profile

\n\n

How to edit profile picture?

\n 1. Move the mouse over to your name from the right corner and select “Profile” from the drop-down menu.\n 2. Select “Upload Profile Picture” from the right.\n 3. Choose a picture to upload from your computer.\n\n

Why having a profile picture is important?

\n Having a profile pic gives you a visual identity that others can recognize at an event and helps them remember you so that you can keep in touch easily.\n\n

What are verifications?

\n Verifications are ways to connect your Plenry profile to other information both online and offline. We offer multiple methods of verification, such as Facebook, government issued ID, Edu account and Google+ account. Having more forms of verification on your Plenry profile helps to build a safe community.\n\n

Who can see my profile information?

\n If you send in a request to attend an event, the host will be able to see your profile, including your name, self-introduction, the verifications that you have completed and past reviews if you have any.\n\n

How to get verifications?

\n To add a verification: click on your name from the upper right corner, go to Profile tab, click on Trust & Verification on the left. Email address: confirm your email from the email we send upon registration. Facebook: connect your Facebook profile to your account. Phone number: receive SMS or call, and then enter the code. Google+ Account: Link your Google account. Government issued ID.\n\n

What is ID verification?

\n At Plenry, we take trust and safety seriously. We offer ID verification through patent technology to help us build a safer platform and community. Most hosts only accept guests who have completed ID verification for safety purposes. During the ID verification process, you will be asked to take a picture of your government issued ID using a web camera or by uploading one from your computer. Upon completing the process, there will be an indicator on your profile that shows that you\'ve done so.\n\n

Is ID verification secure?

\n Your information collected from Plenry is transmitted using SSL with secure encryption. Our third-party partners save all information from ID verification in accordance with our Privacy Policy. Only a small number of Plenry employees are allowed to access to the original documents you provide strictly for trouble shooting purposes.Your original document will never be shared with other users of the platform.\n\n

4 Hosting an Event

\n
\n\n

Top tips for successfully hosting an event.

\n 1. Create attractive and interesting listings. To attract potential guests create a detailed listing that shows what you have planned for the event. Highlight the theme and whatever else you have to offer.\n
\n 2. Set a competitive price to attract guests. You can decide the price you want to charge to each guest. Plenry charges an additional 15% fee to guests for using the platform.\n
\n 3. Respond quickly. As a host, you’ll receive a notification that a guest has sent in a request or a question. You can respond by logging onto the Plenry website. Click on the “Hosting” tab and select “Booking Requests” from the left menu. Accept or reject guests based on their public profile.\n
\n 4. While getting ready for the event, check your guest list after your event is closed for booking.\n
\n 5. Avoid rejection and canceling. While hosts are free to reject any guest they wish, it is best to accept everyone, unless there is a strong reason not to. Canceling an event without a strong reason looks bad, and is not advised.\n\n

How much should I charge for an event?

\n The rate is completely determined by you. If you do not have any reviews on the platform, we suggest that you charge a little bit towards the lower end to attract more guests.\n\n \n \n \n\n

How to be a good host?

\n Respond quickly to each guest\'s requests once you receive them. Increase your acceptance rate if possible. Respond to any questions guests may have in a timely manner. Print a guest list after your listing is closed to booking. Be friendly to all guests and enjoy the event.\n\n

How do I decide whether I should accept the guest?

\n We highly recommend you to check each guest\'s profile before accepting them. We also suggest you accept guests who have completed more verifications and those whose profiles look authentic to you\n\n

How do I get paid?

\n First you need to add a Payout Method to your account. Click your name from the upper right corner. Click on Setting > Payout Preference > Add Payout Preference Please choose a Payout method to get paid.\n
\n Payout methods include: Direct deposit, PayPal, Mailed Check\n\n

How often will I get paid?

\n We issue funds collected within 1 business day after the event date to your account.\n\n

What is the refund policy?

\n After your booking is confirmed, we issue full refunds if you cancel 72 hours prior to the event. We do not issue refunds if you cancel within 72 hours.\n\n
